PRISONER'S GUIDE

How to Escape:

1. Guess the hidden word by selecting letters to avoid execution.

2. Each incorrect guess adds a part to the hangman. Wrong guesses allowed:

  • Easy: 8 attempts
  • Medium: 7 attempts
  • Hard: 6 attempts

3. Use hints when you're stuck - but use them wisely as they reduce your final score.

4. Complete the word before the hangman is fully drawn to escape.

Hangman Sequence:

1. Rope appears (always visible)

2. Head appears (1st wrong guess)

3. Body appears (2nd wrong guess)

4. Left arm (3rd wrong guess)

5. Right arm (4th wrong guess)

6. Left leg (5th wrong guess)

7. Right leg (6th wrong guess) - GAME OVER!

Scoring System:

- Each correct letter: +10 points

- Each hint used: -25 points

- Winning without hints: Bonus +50 points

- Each remaining life: +15 points

- Time bonus: +1 point per second remaining
